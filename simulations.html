The simulations suggests that leveraging the discrete Schr¨odinger bridge oﬀers a practical
advantage, we can push a ﬁnite set of particles through an empirical approximation of the SB
scheme. The aggregate distribution of these particles then provides a discrete-time approxi-
mation of the heat ﬂow.
